About Me

I love to build
things from the
ground up.

Ever since I can remember, I have always enjoyed creating things from scratch. This has led me to work as both a professional musician and as a chef, where I have always enjoyed making things that make people happier and allow me to express myself in a creative and tangible way. My first encounter with coding immediately ignited a desire to pursue web development as a career, and this desire stems from the same drive to create things from scratch.
