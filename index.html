About Me

I love to build things from the ground up.

Ever since I can remember, I have always enjoyed creating things from scratch. This lead me to careers as both a passionate musician and as a chef, where I could create things that make people happier and allow me to express myself in a creative and tangible way. My first encounter with coding immediately ignited a desire to pursue web development as a career, and this desire stems from the same drive to create things from scratch.
